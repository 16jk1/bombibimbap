    RECIPES

    Our Bibimbap services in a sizzling stone bowl with steamed rice, sauteed zucchini, carrot, boiled beet, kale, red cabage (in an apple, onion, grape juice), burdock, mushroom, quinoa, and marinated white cabbage.

    Why is BOM Bibimbap

    special?

  • 한끼: A meal
  • Healthy and fulfilling meal holds so much value in Korean culture. BOM Bibimbap’s goal is to offer people in Toronto their 한끼, made with care and passion.

    • We incorporated traditional Korean hot sauce and created a fusion bibimbap dish by combining traditional aspects of the dish with local vegetables. Making it friendlier for the community.
