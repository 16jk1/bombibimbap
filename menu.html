Stone Bowl Bibimbap

About

Our Bibimbap services in a sizzling stone bowl with steamed rice, sautéed zucchini & carrot, boiled beat, kale, red cabbage (in an apple, onion, grape juice), potato, romaine, & quinoa and marinated white cabbage. *

Instructions

STEP 1: Choose your favorite bibimbap and toppings
STEP 2: Add the sauce to your bibimbap
STEP 3: Stir everything, while enjoying the sound of sizzling
STEP 4: Serve with spoon or chopsticks
STEP 5: Eat Smart! Stay Health!
*also available in regular bowl (off $1.00)
*may change with season
